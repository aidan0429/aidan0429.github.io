Introduction

From its creation, the web has drastically changed and evolved, however one thing has stayed constant, the people creating the changes. The goal of this website is to highlight a new change in website creation, Universal Design and Accessibility.

Overview

Universal Design in the concept of creating websites in order to allow the most accessability possible. This means creating websites that can be used by people of all demographics, all around the world.
